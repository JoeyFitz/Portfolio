About Me


A native of Long Island, NY, I left home to join the Navy when I was 17. I served on Active Duty for many years as a helicopter pilot and had the privilege of meeting great people in awesome places around the globe. I transitioned into tech in 2018 when I joined Veritone, Inc. From Customer Success to Sales Engineering, I strive to grow more technically proficient in the SaaS industry every day. I have a B.S. from the U.S. Naval Academy, an MBA from the Naval Postgraduate School and am currently enrolled in a coding bootcamp at UCSD. I proudly call San Diego home alongside my wife Kathleen. We're eagerly awaiting the arrival of our first child in August. Check out my work below and feel free to reach out via the contact info provided. Thanks for stopping by!
